My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Bahubali

Bahubali is an epic movie of Indian history. Its a visual treat to watch

Pushpa

Pushpa is an amazing movie of Indian Origin.Its about rarely available Red sandalwood and how it is transported to foreign countries to make different products available in market.The movie is well known for the character PUSHPA for its outstanding swag.

RRR

RRR is a fictional movie of two freedom fighters of India who belongs to the same time period of freedom fight.The movie is about bringing those two freedom fighters together as a brotherhood and showing beautiful visuals of each of the characters
